## Reading

- The **bookmaker line is the ceiling**: closing odds aggregate vastly more information than public match results; matching them with public data alone would be extraordinary, and we do not claim it.
- The honest test for FUT-K's machinery is the ordering: **naive baseline < our rating models <= market**. If our Elo / attack-defense Poisson sit between the naive baseline and the market, the probabilistic machinery is sane and externally anchored.
- These are *match-outcome* models built on the engine's Poisson core; the in-play panel (goal-in-window) has no public baseline to compare against, which is exactly why this external anchor uses the 1X2 target.
